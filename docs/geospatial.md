# Geospatial Definition: Kentucky Study Area

The Kentucky project analyzes highway disaster risk statewide, focusing on key infrastructure points.

## Priority Highways
- **I-64:** East-West corridor through Louisville, Frankfort, and Lexington.
- **I-65:** North-South corridor through Louisville, Elizabethtown, and Bowling Green.
- **I-75:** North-South corridor through Lexington and Richmond.

## High-Risk Features
- **Bridges & Overpasses:** High-priority "icing blackspots" due to surface temperature drops.
- **Urban HIN:** High Injury Networks as identified by Vision Zero Louisville.
