# Kentucky Highway Disasters Risk Map

This project aims to create a comprehensive risk map of highway disasters, with a focus on road icing and winter weather hazards, across the state of Kentucky, USA.

## Project Goal
To collect, clean, and structure data regarding road safety incidents, icing blackspots (including bridges/overpasses), and highway infrastructure vulnerability to aid in research and safety improvements.

## Authentic Data Sources
- **KYTC Open Data:** Highway Information System (HIS) and Snow/Ice Priority Routes.
- **Kentucky State Police:** Public Crash Data (KSP CRASH database).
- **Kentucky Transportation Center (KTC):** Annual High-Crash Location Reports.

## Geospatial Scope
Statewide analysis with focus on high-traffic Interstates (I-64, I-65, I-75) and identified high-injury networks in urban areas like Louisville.
