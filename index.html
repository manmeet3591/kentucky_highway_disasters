Research Context

The Chandigarh Highway Disasters Risk Map focuses on the intersection of weather-induced hazards and infrastructure vulnerability. Our model prioritizes fog-related visibility failures and monsoon structural risks.

Data Attribution: Risk markers and incident hotspots are sourced extensively from regional news reports, traffic archives, and social media event detection.
